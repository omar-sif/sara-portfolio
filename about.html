ABOUT ME

I'm Sara Elaam, a graphic designer currently completing a Master's degree at BAU Centro Universitario de Artes y Diseño. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design from ESAV in Marrakech, Morocco, where I developed a strong foundation in visual identity, editorial design, and concept-driven projects.

My practice is rooted in research and storytelling, exploring how design can carry meaning, culture, and intention. I'm curious, detail-oriented, and drawn to projects that question, connect, and communicate.
